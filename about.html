About US

Government Polytechnic College of Ranchi (Jharkhand) is a prestigious institution spreaded in a wide area of land at the heart of the city, established in 1954, is known for its academic excellence with a wide range of Engineering programs that is CSE, ECE, EE, Mechanical and civil, Experienced faculty, unparallel infrastructure and Emphasis to inspire and unlock creativity with liberating technology, the strong focus on technical and soft skills training to enhance employability of students. The college provide a comprehensive education. It also offers a vibrant campus life, encouraging extracurricular activities and interactions with different quarters of the society that helps in shaping students into competent professionals.

  • Core values -
      1.Team Spirit and bonding
      Government Polytechnic Ranchi is an extended family where students are integrated into one family with a closed Bond and Team Spirit.
      2. academic excellences
      Through quality, value-based cost-effective education of excellence and relevance to contemporary needs, accessible to all section of society.
      3. selfless service
      By developing values creating willingness and capacity to work with one's hands, promoting respect for dignity of labour and spirit of self-sacrifice to serve humanity.
      4. Social responsibility
      Through Awareness of Democratic values and freedom, respect of the rights of others, awareness of duties and obligations to society, including growth, empowering weaker and marginalised class and reaching to the last and to the lost to the contribute to national development.
      5. Critical thinking
      We embrace and encourage thinking critically about the world's problems, we teach students how to learn and and be analytical so they can go out into the world as lifelong learners to solve the problems of the 21st century.
      6. Doing best
      We always strive to work on ourselves and achieve our personal best in the classroom.
      7. Open mindness
      We are open to new experiences and new challenges. We are willing to change our minds when faced with new evidence.
      8. Perseverance
      We recognise that learning is hard but our students try to reach the learning goals under the guidance of the teachers.

    • Our Mission

      The mission objective of GPR is to provide value based, comprehensive education to inculcate a value based complete person, whose hallmarks are intellectual strength, emotional maturity, truthfulness, simple living and high thinking scientific temper with good moral character who discharges duties and obligations and capable of giving a full response to social and environmental challenges.

    Our Vission

    GPR's vision of innovative comprehensive, interdisciplinary and value-based education that enhances academic excellence with holistic development, the strategic plan is a road map for providing an environment to produce students who are ready to the take on challenges and be a leader with fine blend of top-quality Academic's work experience and a strong value system.
